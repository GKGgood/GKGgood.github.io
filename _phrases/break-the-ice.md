---
title: break the ice
meaning: 打破僵局，活跃气氛
definition: To say or do something that helps people feel more relaxed in a social situation.
phonetic: 
part_of_speech: phrase
examples:
  - A simple joke can break the ice at a meeting.
  - He asked an easy question to break the ice.
---

This phrase is useful when people feel awkward or do not know how to start talking to each other.
