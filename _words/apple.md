---
title: apple
meaning: 苹果
definition: A round fruit with red, green, or yellow skin and firm white flesh.
phonetic: /ˈae.pəl/
part_of_speech: noun
examples:
  - I eat an apple every morning.
  - The apple on the table looks fresh.
---

Apple is a very common beginner word. You can use it when talking about fruit, food, or healthy habits.
