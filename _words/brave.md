---
title: brave
meaning: 勇敢的
definition: Showing courage and a willingness to face danger, difficulty, or pain.
phonetic: /breiv/
part_of_speech: adjective
examples:
  - She was brave enough to speak in public.
  - It is brave to admit your mistakes.
---

Brave is often used to describe someone who faces difficulty, danger, or fear with confidence.
